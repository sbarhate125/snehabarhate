What is Digital Marketing?

Digital marketing is the promotion of products or services using digital channels such as search engines, social media, email, and websites. It helps businesses connect with customers where they spend most of their timeâ€”online.

Key Areas of Digital Marketing

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Social Media Marketing (SMM)
  • Pay-Per-Click Advertising (PPC)
  • Content Marketing
  • Email Marketing
  • Affiliate Marketing

Digital marketing is essential for modern businesses because it offers measurable results, precise targeting, and cost-effective campaigns compared to traditional marketing methods.

Top Career Roles in Digital Marketing

1. SEO Specialist

Focuses on optimizing websites to achieve higher rankings on search engines like Google.

2. Social Media Manager

Manages brand presence and engagement on social media platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and LinkedIn.

3. Content Marketer

Creates valuable, consistent content to attract and retain a target audience.

4. Email Marketing Specialist

Builds email campaigns to drive conversions and customer loyalty.

5. PPC Manager

Manages paid advertising campaigns to increase visibility and drive traffic.
